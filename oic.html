What is
Disarmament and International Security Committee?

Although several attempts to bond Muslims were sporadic and incoherent, yet they contributed to creating the Organization of the Islamic Conference in 1969, which was renamed as “Organization of Islamic Cooperation” in the UN to delve into the deep geopolitical discussions in the Islamic World. The OIC considers itself as the “collective voice of the Muslim world” and endeavors to safeguard the concerns of its member states which stems from the belief Page | 5 that OIC came as a culmination of Muslim struggles to establish an institution for joint Islamic action and solidarity. OIC, with a single item on its agenda, has transformed into a large intergovernmental organization with an expanded agenda covering issues ranging from peace to alleviating poverty and combating diseases and terrorism, and thus UEMCON'21 chooses it as its final committee for the event.
