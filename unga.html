What is
Disarmament and International Security Committee?

The world now stands on the edge of continuous international crisis and tensions where the dominance stays for land , power and authority over life not from now, but ages. As history records, in 1946, when the world war remained aghast and two Japanese states namely, Hiroshima and Nagasaki were devastated with nuclear bombs, the earliest resolution was drafted by the First Committee of the UNGA, demanding for the "Establishment of a commission to deal with problems raised by the discovery of Atomic Energy." Under the Chapter IV of the United Nations Charter, it was then a council was established called the Disarmament and International Security Committee (DISEC), to ensure the redressal of grievances andanger, thus maintaining international peace and security. Thus we, the team of UEMCON'21 officially declare DISEC as our first committee, revealed. Step on the floor and preach the world your diplomatic ethics by being a part of the largest committee of our Conclave of Nations 2021.
